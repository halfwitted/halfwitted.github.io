Peter Lindbergh

In a world where everything is post-processed and where digital manipulation has become an inseperable part of
photography,there are few artists who refuse to polish their images. Peter Lindbergh is one of them, He is know for his
authentic,soulful and natural-looking portraits. Through his work he introduced a new concept of beauty:raw and realistic.
Due to this achievement, he's considered to be among the pioneers in contemprary photography.
