Sebastiao Salgado

Sebastiao Salgado is, without a shadow of a doubt, one of the greatest living photographers.  He mainly works in the field
of social documentry, portraying humanity during some of its most devastating stages.  salgado has published many books
over the cours of his nearly 50-year career, but some of the most iconic are Geenesis, Other americas,workers and Terra.To
create his images, he has travelled all over the world and has visited more than 120 countries.In 2014, a documentry movie
about Salgado's life was released. It is called" The Salt of the Earth", directed by Wim Wenders, one of the most renowned
European directors, and juliano Ribeiro Salgado, Sebastiao Salgado's son himself.
